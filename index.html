A bit about professional me

I have 3 years experience as a software engineer, working in both small companies and large organizations. I get up to speed quickly in whatever tech the problem at demands and I enjoy being able to quickly master the right tool for the problem. I think the variety of tech in my work history reflects that capacity (needs must). Saying that, I really enjoy creating beautiful, powerful and user-friendly front-ends and aim to grow my abilities in that area in the coming years.

At the Irish SME I worked for, I developed a cross platform application using Ionic3/Angular5 from scratch. This application is now the flagship app of the company. I also wrote the iOS versions for two small applications. I developed a number of libraries for the company’s .NET backend. I continue to provide maintenance services for this company.

I completed a six month month contract with King in Barcelona as part of my master's degree. While there I developed the backend API for a new game feature (Java), I also worked on their in-house management tools, using AngularJS and Spring Hibernate.

Prior to this I worked as a data analyst for SSE Airtricity, a role which included data retrieval, task automation and dashboard construction using Python, Excel and other in-house tools. I built a web platform for managing regulatory submissions using Python Flask, greatly increasing the ease of which submissions could be managed and dealt with.

In 2019 I graduated with a Masters in Computer Science from University College Dublin achieving a GPA of 3.77 (90%).
